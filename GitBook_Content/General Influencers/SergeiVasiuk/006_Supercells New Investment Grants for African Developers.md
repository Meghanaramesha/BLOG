### [Supercell’s New Investment Grants for African Developers](https://www.gamigion.com/supercells-new-investment-grants-for-african-developers/)

Mobile gaming giant Supercell announced a new developer grant program specifically for African studios during the recent MaliyoCon conference in Nigeria. This initiative aims to provide non-dilutive funding ranging from $20,000 to $200K to support the continent's emerging talent, marking a significant investment in what many see as the world's fastest-growing gaming market. Maliyocon 2025 The announcement reflects a broader shift in Supercell's strategy to cultivate long-term partnerships worldwide. While specific details of the African grants are new, they align with the company's recently re-launched "Supercell Investments" arm, which focuses on backing creative, independent teams globally. A Focus on Founders and Long-Term Vision At the heart of Supercell's investment philosophy is a focus on empowering founders who share their core values: high ambition, creative independence, and a dedication to building "forever games".
