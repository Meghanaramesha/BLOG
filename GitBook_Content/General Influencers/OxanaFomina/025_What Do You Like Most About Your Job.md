### [What Do You Like Most About Your Job?](https://www.gamigion.com/what-do-you-like-most-about-your-job/)

I really enjoy finding cause-and-effect relationships in player behavior, discovering insights in data, exploring games, and identifying triggers and call-to-actions that motivate players to engage, return, and pay. I love learning not just why a game/event/offer evokes certain emotions and feelings, but most importantly, because of what? I am fascinated by deconstructing the gameplay and events to understand the secret to a game's success. I am incredibly inspired when I see the beauty and pattern of engagement and monetization mechanics. I sincerely admire the work that the game creators have done, and I often want to meet and learn about those who have created a particular event or mechanic in a game.
