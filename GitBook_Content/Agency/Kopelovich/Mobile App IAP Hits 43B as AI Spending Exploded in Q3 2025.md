### [Mobile App IAP Hits $43B as AI Spending Exploded in Q3 2025](https://www.gamigion.com/mobile-app-iap-hits-43b-as-ai-spending-exploded-in-q3-2025/)

Mobile app spending kept climbing in Q3 2025, with global IAP revenue hitting $43.2B, up 11% YoY. The market is now brushing against the $50B quarterly milestone. Data: SensorTower More Highlights? Non-games extended their lead with $22.3B, powered by AI. GenAI spend exploded from $400M to nearly $1.5B YoY, now 7% of all non-game revenue. Mobile games held steady at $20.9B, their second-best quarter ever. Both stores grew fast. iOS rose 10% and kept its grip on non-game spend at 72%. Google Play jumped 14.5% and continues accelerating. AI reshaped the revenue charts. ChatGPT leaped from #16 to #2 globally, sitting right behind TikTok and hitting #1 in markets like Turkey, Brazil, Spain, and Canada.
