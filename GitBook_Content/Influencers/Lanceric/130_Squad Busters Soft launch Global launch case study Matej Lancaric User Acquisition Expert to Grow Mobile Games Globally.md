**What is Squad Busters?**

Squad Busters is Supercell‚Äôs new mobile game that brings together characters from all their previous titles, creating an exciting multiverse where players can engage in battle arenas. This unique concept merges popular characters and gameplay elements from games like Clash of Clans, Brawl Stars, and more into one action-packed experience.Imagine a multiverse of all of Supercell‚Äôs previously released games. And the characters from these games all converged into one single battle arena. This is Squad Busters.

The new mobile action game offers a unique PvP/PvE experience in which players ‚Äúbuild and bust‚Äù Squads comprised of various characters from Supercell‚Äôs catalog of games, including Clash of Clans, Clash Royale, Brawl Stars, and more.

Players begin with just a single character but can unlock additional characters to join their Squad by exploring and fighting on the map.

![](./images/https3A2F2Fsubstack-post-medias3amazonawscom2Fpublic2Fimages2F70423cc7-a8d5-41e6-8868-15b65c5e94f8_2436x1125_186062f5.png)

As they earn Coins, they can open chests that unlock these Characters and upgrade them to become more powerful.

Combat is mostly automatic, although players control the general movement of their squad and can launch some special abilities and spells.

## How did the reroll mechanic and character merging enhance gameplay in Squad Busters‚Äô second beta test?

Squad Busters finished its 2nd beta test and it‚Äôs a great game design exercise looking at the changes between the two versions. The main challenges in the first version were repetitiveness of the main mode, lack of depth during gameplay and too much luck factor in the first pick of the characters.¬†I was able to play the game during this beta, we even recorded a podcast about it!

The luck factor was nicely solved with the addition of a reroll mechanic, where players can use a consumable to get refresh on a choice of 3 characters once per match. This is especially critical in the very first pick of the match, as the first character determines how fast you can build up your squad compared to other players. It can still end up being a lackluster starting character, but by giving players the choice to reroll, the frustration is definitely mitigated to some degree.

![](./images/https3A2F2Fsubstack-post-medias3amazonawscom2Fpublic2Fimages2F48b3201e-bfd5-4f51-97db-6107e5d44385_922x624_c836ce98.png)

As you collect more and more characters throughout the match, you start to form strategies around those that were very straightforward before. Now, they borrowed the merging mechanic from the auto-chess genre, where if you collect 3 of the same characters, they merge into a stronger one than the previous version.

Not only does the new character has more health than 3 combined and has an ability bonus, but you also lower your character count as every other character costs more gold per chest, which actually goes down with the merge. So now you have some interesting choices/ risks to take during choosing your character setup during gameplay.

![](./images/https3A2F2Fsubstack-post-medias3amazonawscom2Fpublic2Fimages2Fa2960d3e-4baa-465f-b0e3-8d80db5079a1_932x650_1c1c375a.png)

## What are the new character progression mechanics and monetization strategies in the soft launch of Squad Busters?

The soft launch process for mobile games in 2024 has evolved with new approaches, although the core framework remains similar. Developers now focus more on data-driven strategies, refined marketing efforts, and enhanced user testing to ensure a smoother global release. These changes address modern player expectations and competition in the mobile gaming market, making the soft launch phase more crucial than ever.

Character progression was radically changed. Currently, you merge these 4 stages of shards, starting from Baby form, going all the way to Ultra with a 10 to 1 merge conversion ratio.

They are upgraded through just shards and give you portal energy for the Squad journey. You can also buy a lot of shards in the shop and even unlock completely new characters.

![](./images/https3A2F2Fsubstack-post-medias3amazonawscom2Fpublic2Fimages2Fcb7aa590-ce60-40de-bc04-aeedbb5e429c_1280x750_c0e82e17.jpeg)

The progression gating mechanic is chest tickets, which you need to use to open a chest. Otherwise, you can play without getting rewards. Chest tickets are the best currency sink in the game currently.

The Star Drop system from Brawl Stars was also added to the game, proving that Gacha mechanics reign supreme. This is powering the chest opening now, where each click has a chance to upgrade the chest rarity. This also nicely connects to the streak mechanic, whereby being in the top 5 gives you a direct bonus in the form of better chances of chest rarity upgrade and more shards. This essentially makes the streak saver‚Äôs second-best gold sink in the game. On the other hand, I almost threw my phone against the wall when I lost my streak due to my stupid mistake in the game. ### How does the decoration mechanic in Squad Busters enhance resource management in gameplay?

The decoration mechanic in Squad Busters enhances resource management by allowing players to strategically place decorations that provide various benefits, such as increasing resource generation and improving squad performance.

In the 2nd Beta version, the decoration/building mechanic also allowed players to spend hammer‚Äôs currency (found during battles) on cosmetic and resource buildings.
