Summary
In this episode, the hosts delve into the evolving landscape of the gaming industry, focusing on CPI testing, game mechanics, and market trends. They discuss the impact of new game launches, the disruption of traditional genres like Solitaire, and the potential of branded games such as Barbie. The conversation also touches on the strategies of various game developers, including Century Games and Voodoo, and explores the future of mobile gaming amidst changing consumer preferences.
Takeaways

CPI testing has evolved with mini games becoming the new standard.
The gaming industry is experiencing significant disruptions, particularly in the Solitaire category.
Innovations in game mechanics are crucial for standing out in a crowded market.
Branded games like Barbie have potential but face challenges in execution.
Voodoo continues to innovate within the casual gaming space.
Century Games is a company to watch due to their strategic approach.
The Forex genre is becoming saturated with similar onboarding mechanics.
Post-apocalyptic themes are gaining traction in mobile games.
The importance of user acquisition strategies cannot be overstated.
The future of mobile gaming will likely see more integration of successful mechanics from various genres.
