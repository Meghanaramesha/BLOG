Summary
In this episode, the hosts delve into the latest trends in ad monetization within the gaming industry. The discussion includes detailed analyses of specific games, their gameplay mechanics, and revenue strategies, showcasing the evolving landscape of mobile gaming and the rise of ultra casual games, particularly from regions like Vietnam.
