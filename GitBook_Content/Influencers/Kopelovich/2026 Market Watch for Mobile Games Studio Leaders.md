‚Ä¢ The ‚Äúbig buyer‚Äù not firing checks every quarter changes late-stage studio behavior across the board.

### Kingshot‚Äôs $500M Funnel: A Playbook Every Studio Should Study

**The breakthrough:**

**Why this matters:**

‚Ä¢ 4X monetizes extremely well.  
‚Ä¢ 4X also has some of the highest CPIs on mobile.  
‚Ä¢ A casual-style first session flips the economics of the genre.

‚Ä¢ US accounts for 42% of total spend

### UA Dynamics: AppLovin‚Äôs iOS Climb & Remarketing Shift

#### AppLovin is quietly catching Apple on iOS

‚Ä¢ Privacy changes leveling the field  
‚Ä¢ AppLovin‚Äôs creative and ML edge  
‚Ä¢ Apple‚Äôs limited ad inventory vs AppLovin‚Äôs full-network footprint

### The Bottom Line for Studio Leaders

The macro picture is tightening while the operational picture is getting more innovative.

**The winners in 2026‚Äì2027 will be the studios that:**

‚Ä¢ Treat onboarding as a UA channel  
‚Ä¢ Blend casual acquisition with midcore or casino monetization
