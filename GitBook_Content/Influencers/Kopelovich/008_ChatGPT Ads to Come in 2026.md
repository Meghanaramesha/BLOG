### What We Know So Far

#### Rollout

Closed beta in 2025. Global launch for free users in 2026.

#### Pricing

Charged per click or action.  
No impression-based inflation.

#### Placement

Ads appear **inside** the conversation.  
Native to the reply. No pop-ups.

#### Targeting

Semantic intent, session context and user memory (if enabled).  
No keyword-era targeting.

#### User Control

Users can disable memory to reduce personalization.

#### Formats

* Sponsored answers.
* Promoted GPTs.
* Contextual tool suggestions.
* Action buttons blended into replies.

#### Attribution

Session tokens or conversion APIs. No cookies.

*AI becomes an intent capture layer that lives upstream of Google.*
